Onboarding Step 3: Body Type Assessment

Endomorph

Curvy & Fuller Silhouette

Slower metabolic rate, naturally stores more fat, wider waist-to-hip ratio, and retains solid bone mass.

Mesomorph

Toned & Athletic Silhouette

Efficient metabolic rate, retains lean muscle easily, symmetrical bone structure, and responsive to fitness routines.

Ectomorph

Slim & Slender Silhouette

Ultra-fast metabolic rate, hyper-active calorie burning, narrow bone frame, and naturally lower body fat storage.
